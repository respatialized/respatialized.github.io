Design Ethos

SITUATED PERSPECTIVE

A landscape, cityscape, or structure must be visible from the perspective of a human being within a scene. Aerial perspectives are situated in an aerial vehicle.

Some implications of this become clear immediately: - if there is a helicopter observing an area, the question comes to mind: why? who is the observer observing? to what end? - is a structure built for repair? can someone get to the vantage point in the scene? what tools or affordances exist for those purposes? - a "non-realistic" viewpoint can be achieved through a screen or other diagnostic device
